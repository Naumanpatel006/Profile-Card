Exploring Munich, Germany

Munich captured my heart with its mix of old-world charm and modern flair. From the stunning architecture of Marienplatz to the peaceful vibes of the English Garden, every corner had a story. I wandered through bustling beer gardens, tried delicious Bavarian cuisine, and even caught a local music performance. A city that’s both calm and energetic — Munich is a must-visit!
